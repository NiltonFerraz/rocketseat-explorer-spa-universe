The Universe

The word Universe derives from the Latin universum. The Latin word was used by Cicero and later by others authors with the same meaning that is currently used. The Latin word is derived from the poetic contraction unvorsum — first used by Lucretius in Book IV of his “On the Nature of Things” — which connects un, uni (one) with vorsum, versum (meaning "something rotated, rolled or changed").
